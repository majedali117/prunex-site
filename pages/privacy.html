We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

If we transfer your personal information to countries outside of your jurisdiction, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
