About Prunex

Prunex exists to help organisations adopt AI with stronger control, clearer accountability, and greater trust. We build governance infrastructure that makes AI safer to deploy, simpler to oversee, and easier to prove compliant.

Our Mission

Making AI governance operational

We help enterprises govern AI with confidence by providing structured policy control, continuous compliance support, and responsible AI oversight. Our mission is to turn governance from a set of principles into an operational discipline that works at enterprise scale.

Our Vision

AI at scale, without compromise

We envision a future where organisations use AI at scale without sacrificing policy, trust, compliance, or responsibility. Where governance is not a barrier to innovation but the foundation that makes it sustainable, accountable, and worthy of confidence.

Why We Exist

The problem we are solving

The AI adoption curve is outpacing the governance infrastructure needed to support it.

01

AI adoption is accelerating

Enterprises are deploying AI models, agents, and workflows across every function. The pace is increasing, but governance structures remain static and manual.

02

Governance is fragmented

Policies sit in documents. Oversight is team-by-team. There is no single layer ensuring consistent control across AI systems, data flows, and decision points.

03

Enterprises need control

Regulators expect proof. Boards expect accountability. Customers expect transparency. Organisations need infrastructure that delivers all three, continuously.
